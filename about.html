Who is Cardiostasis?

Cardiostasis is a band formed in 2022 by 4 Highschool best Friends in Capelle/Rotterdam, consisting of Jonathan martosoewondo, Jeffrey Li (Jeff stasis), Alex (aaron) Kokkee and Koen Erkeland. The band started out playing covers from popular bands such as Metallica, Nirvana, Radiohead and Foo Fighters but have grown into playing heavier and a more refined sound, each member adopting their own influences.

In 2023 Jonathan martosoewondo got kicked out of the band, due to growing tensions within the band.

In 2025 Cardiostasis Had their first Live Performance and covered the song MakeDamnSure by Taking Back Sunday

Cardiostasis has had a rough upcoming, but its always been a team effort, we feel like teamwork is the essence of playing in a good band. Communication is key, especially when playing for an audience. Playing a song live Lights up your body and soul in ways that you could never imagine. Now playing your own song doubles that feeling. To play your own song for an audience is just an outer world experience, to have created a track and playing it for people that are loving to see every second of it, is our dream...

"Cardiostasis is more than just a band, Its a brotherhood, if one of us wouldnt be here, none of us would be here."
