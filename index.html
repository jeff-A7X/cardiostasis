About Cardiostasis...

Hi, we're Cardiostasis, an Alternative Metal band from Rotterdam. Formed in 2023 by a group of high school friends, we make energetic & emotionally charged music that hits hard. Our sound fuses the raw energy of hardcore punk, metalcore and shoegaze. Stay tuned for our next album coming in 2026.
