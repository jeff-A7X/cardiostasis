About Cardiostasis...

Hi, we're Cardiostasis, an alternative rock band from Rotterdam. Formed in 2023 by a group of high school friends, we make loud, emotionally charged music that hits hard. Our sound fuses the raw energy of punk, metal and hard rock. In May 2025 we released our first EP containing tracks like 'The Cage' and 'Subterfuge'.
